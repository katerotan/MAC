ABOUT METHOD

Metaphoric Associative Cards as a Profound Tool for Psychologist's Work Metaphoric Associative Cards (abbreviated as MAC) are a set of images depicting people, their interactions, life situations, landscapes, animals, household items, and abstract images. The images presented on the cards become a visible metaphor for our values, fears, desires, or associations related to our inner experience. It is important that everyone can imagine and feel something personal. MAM belongs to projective techniques that bring up from the depths of our subconscious those impressions that determine our reaction to various life situations. Projection is a psychological mechanism through which an individual attributes their own traits, emotions, and relationships to someone else. Metaphor is a word or expression used by the client in a figurative sense.
